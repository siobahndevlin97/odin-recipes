Brownies

Description

Brownies are the world's best dessert. Easy to make, easy to bake, and easy to eat.
Personally I enjoy mine with a side of vanilla ice cream on the side.
I find that if you stick them in the fridge overnight, you can enjoy delicious, chilled brownies in the morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 0.5 cup vegetable oil
  • 0.33 cup whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 0.25 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 0.25 teaspoon baking powder
  • 0.5 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325Â°F and line a 9x9-inch baking pan with parchment paper; set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar, vegetable oil, milk, egg, and vanilla until combined.
  3. Whisk in the flour, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder, and chocolate chips until fully combined.
  4. Transfer the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread out evenly with a rubber spatula.
  5. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until the edges are firm and the center does not jiggle.
  6. Allow to cool in the pan before cutting and serving or enjoy warm with ice cream.
