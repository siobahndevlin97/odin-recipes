Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish most known to be Garfield's favorite food. It is also my favorite food and I also happen to be fond of naps.
Lasagna is made up of pasta, tomato sauce, ground beef, garlic, and cheese. It can also be thought of as spaghetti cake.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 0.5 cup parmesan cheese
  • 0.5 pound lean ground beef
  • 0.5 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 0.25 cup fresh parsely
  • 1 egg beaten

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F. Cook pasta al dente according to package directions. Rinse under cold water and set aside.
  2. Brown beef, sausage, onion and garlic over medium high heat until no pink remains. Drain any fat.
  3. Stir in pasta sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning. Simmer 5 minutes.
  4. Make Cheese Mixture by combining 1.5 cups mozzarella, 0.25 cup parmesan cheese, ricotta, parsley, and egg.
  5. Add 1 cup meat sauce to a 9x13 pan. Top with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with one third of the Cheese Mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce. Repeat twice more. Finish with 3 noodles topped with remaining sauce.
  6. Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes.
  7. Uncover, sprinkle with remaining cheese (2.5 cups mozzarella cheese and 0.25 cup parmesan), and bake an additional 15 minutes or until browned and bubbly. Broil 2-3 minutes if desired.
  8. Rest 10-15 minutes before cutting.
